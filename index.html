Privacy Policy

PO Box Blocker collects only the data necessary to perform its functionality, such as shipping address information during checkout. This data is used solely to detect and prevent PO Box addresses, as configured by the merchant.

We do not store, retain, or share customer data with third parties. All logic is executed securely during the checkout process within the store environment.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding privacy, you may contact us at: support@poboxblocker.com
